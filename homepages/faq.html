FAQ

How do I add a new college to the system?

Navigate to the 'College Management' section in the dashboard, click on 'Add College,' and fill out the required fields. Your changes will be updated across the platform immediately.

How can I update college information?

To update a college, go to 'View Colleges,' select the college you want to edit, and modify the necessary fields. Changes are applied instantly to ensure all related club activities remain accurate.

What is the process for deleting a college?

If a college needs to be removed, select the college from 'View Colleges,' and use the 'Delete' option. Be cautious as this action is irreversible and affects all associated data and activities.

How do I view my profile details?

Your profile can be accessed by clicking your the profile section that can be found at the navigation bar at the top or the left side part of the dashboard. This section displays your user information and system permissions.
